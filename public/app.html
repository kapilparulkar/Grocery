Quick Gestures

Navigate your list faster with these shortcuts:

👈 Swipe left on an item → mark as "Out of stock"
👉 Swipe right on an out-of-stock item → Restock it
⬇️ Pull down from the top → Refresh the list
🛖

Shopping Mode

When you're at the store, switch to Shopping Mode:

✅ Check off items as you pick them up
🔄 Hit "Done" to restock all checked items at once
🔗 Share your list via WhatsApp or any app
🎤

Pro Tips

📝 Notes — add brand or size info to any item
